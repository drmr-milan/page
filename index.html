Tek mu je 16 godina: Darko Dragić objavio roman

He is only 16 years old: Darko Dragic published a novel

09.11.2014

Autor teksa: Anadolija


Na policama banjalučkih knjižara uz renomirane autore nalazi se i roman Darka Dragića. Ništa neobično ne bi bilo u tome da Darko nije učenik drugog razreda Tehničke škole i da svoj prvi roman nije napisao gotovo u dahu.

„Volim čitati, trileri su mi omiljeni žanr pa sam pokušao i vlastitu maštu prenijeti na papir. Tako je svjetlost dana u januaru 2013. godine i ugledao moj roman „Protokol Banja Luka“ smješten na ovaj naš prostor, moj grad. Inspiracija je došla nekako iznenada, priča se slagala sama od sebe“, istakao je u razgovoru za Anadoliju šesnaestogodišnji Darko Dragić.
S osmijehom, onako stidljivim, dječačkim dodaje da u osnovnoj školi baš i nije bio uspješan u pisanju. Nikada, kaže, nije dobio više od četvorke za pismene radove, a onda se sve nekako posložilo. I najavljuje da je pri kraju i s drugim dijelom „Protokola Banja Luka“, koji planira okončati kao trilogiju.
No, sebe jednog dana ne vidi kao pisca, već glumca ili režisera, pa nakon okončane Tehničke škole planira upisati Filmsku akademiju.
„Prvi doticaj s glumom imao sam na kraju osnovne škole kada sam dobio ulogu u školskoj predstavi izvedenoj u Narodnom pozorištu RS. Tada me to privuklo, ta magija. To me i opredijelilo da se priključim gradskom pozorištu Jazavac, gdje sam počeo da učim glumu. Tu sam upoznao nove ljude, stekao prijatelje, odigrao svoju prvu predstavu“, kaže Darko.
Pozornica ga, ističe fascinira, tako da nema tremu. Dodaje da je „sjajan osjećaj kada kroz tekst, ulogu koju interpretira uspijete poruku prenijeti na publiku koja to prihvati i za to nagradi“.
Ali koliko je god je opčinjen glumom ovaj mladić želi se okušati i u filmskoj režiji.
„Prvi, katastrofalni pokušaji da uradim kratke filmove nisu me obeshrabrili, jer sam ih radio potpuno sam. Iz njih sam izvukao pouku da trebam učiti, a u tome su mi pomogli i prijatelji iz Jazavca, pa je tako film „Moguća budućnost“ prikazan i na Sarajevo film festivalu i Festivalu srpske film fantastike u Beogradu“, priča Darko.
Kaže da se uprkos svojim interesovanjima ne razlikuje mnogo od svojih vršnjaka.
„Volim košarku, izlaske i druženja s vršnjacima, ali pisanje, gluma, režija me više zanima. Želio bih studirati u Beču i zato moram mnogo raditi“, skromno dodaje.
A najveću podršku Darko ima od mame Tanje. Profesorica u srednjoj Tehnološkoj školi dobro poznaje mlade ljude i ističe da je važno dobro ih usmjeriti.
„Djeci treba biti oslonac, poticati ih da razvijaju svoju kreativnost, šire vidike, uče obrazuju se. To je mnogo važnije od skupih mobilnih telefona, odjeće s potpisom, ispraznog igranja video igrica “, dodaje Tanja Dragić.
I ako je suditi po njenom Darku zaista je u pravu.

Text author: Anadolija


On the shelves of Banja Luka bookstores with renowned authors, there is a novel by Darko Dragic. It would not be anything unusual that Darko was not a student of the second grade of the Technical School and did not write his first novel almost in breath.

"I like to read, the trillers are my favorite genre so I tried to transfer my imagination to paper. So the light was given in January 2013 and I saw my novel "Protocol of Banja Luka" placed on this space, my city. The inspiration came somehow suddenly, the story rang out of itself, "sixteen-year-old Darko Dragic pointed out in an interview with Anadolu
With a smile, as shy, he adds to the boys that he was not very successful in writing at elementary school. He never said, he received more than four for written works, and then everything sorted. And he announces that at the end and with the other part of the "Banja Luka Protocol", which is planned to end as a trilogy.
But one day he does not see himself as a writer, but an actor or director, and after the end of the Technical School he plans to enroll the Academy of Film.
"I had the first contact with acting at the end of elementary school when I got a role in a school play performed at the National Theater of RS. Then it attracted me, that magic. That made me decide to join the Jazavac city theater, where I began to learn the acting. I met new people there, got friends, played my first show, "says Darko.
The stunt, emphasizes fascinating, so there is no shame. He adds that "it's a great feeling when through the text, the role that you interpret, you can transfer the message to an audience that accepts it and rewards it."
But as much as he is fascinated by the acting, this young man wants to try even in film direction.
"The first, catastrophic attempts to make short films did not discourage me, because I worked them completely alone. I learned from them that I need to learn, and this was also helped by my friends from Jazavac, so the film "Possible Future" is shown at the Sarajevo Film Festival and the Festival of Serbian Film Fiction in Belgrade, "Darko says.
He says that, despite his interests, he does not differ much from his peers.
"I love basketball, going out and hanging out with my peers, but writing, acting, directing me more. I would like to study in Vienna and that's why I have to do a lot, "he added modestly.
And Darko has the biggest support from Tanja, mother. A professor at the Secondary School of Technology knows young people well and stresses that it is important to direct them well.
"Children need to be a support, encourage them to develop their creativity, broader aspects, learn education. This is much more important than expensive mobile phones, signature clothes, empty video games, "adds Tanja Dragic.
And if she is to judge her son, she's really right.

Imam 16 godina, snimam filmove i pišem knjige

I'm 16 years old, I make movies and write books

04.09.2014

Autor teksa: Kontakt portal


U junu su napravili još jedan film koji je dospio na ovogodišnji TeenArena – Sarajevo Film Festival.
“Imamo još dosta planova u budućnosti, ali nemamo sredstava” kaže ovaj učenik Elektrotehničke škole u Banjaluci i kao u nekom filmu, priča:
“Sve se desilo na projekciji filma `Jack Reacher` u bioskopu Palas, kad sam jednostavno dobio želju da i ja uradim neki film. I od tada sam manje gledao, a više radio i snimao. Za sada imamo dva filma. Ljubav prema filmovima sam imao oduvijek. Išao sam po bioskopima, gledao na TV, ali nikad se nisam nadao da bih jednog dana mogao biti režiser. Ali eto dogodilo se. ”
Film Darka Dragića koji je prikazan na tinejdžerskoj konkurenciji Sarajevo film festivala zove se “Moguća budućnost 1”. Radnja se vrti oko momka koji je u jednoj zgradi, a pritom izgubljen u vremenu i prostoru. Na sve načine pokušava saznati sta se zapravo desilo, ali ne ide mu od ruke. Snimljen je i drugi dio filma, a trenutno je u post-produkciji, autori ga najavljuju za 20. novembar.
Radi se post-produkcija, dakle! I scenarista je tu.
“Moj prijatelj Siniša Radusinović je scenarista, i pohađamo školu glume u Gradskom pozorištu `Jazavac`. Mentori, Velimir Blanić i Slađana Zrnic puno pomažu savjetima, kao i režiser Saša Hajduković, koji nam je ukazao greške i takođe dao savjete za buduće projekte”.
Darkina stvaralačka energija nikad ne spava pa je stigao da napiše knjigu po kojoj, naravno, hoće da snimi film. Triler se tove`Protokol: Banjaluka`, i tu je glavni jedan Aleksandar, penzionisani obavještajac. Jednog dana njegov prijatelj Marko ga zamoli da umjesto njega ode u London po neke nove projekte. Ovaj pristane, ali u Londonu mu se desi automobilska nesreća, gdje gubi identitet, supruga mu nestaje…
”Trenutno pisem 2. dio, i planiram da ga objavim najkasnije 2016. godine. Volio bih naći sponzore i snimiti film po svojoj knjizi, ali tu treba puno posla. Naravno ja se tome nadam u skorijoj budućnosti!“ zaključuje Darko Dragić koji planira da studira filmsku režiju u Beču.

Text author: Kontakt portal


In June, they made another film that came to this year's TeenArena - Sarajevo Film Festival.

"We still have plenty of plans in the future, but we do not have the resources," says this student at the Electrical School in Banja Luka and as in a film, he says:
"Everything happened on the screening of the` Jack Reacher 'movie at the Palas cinema, when I simply got the desire to do a movie. And since then, I've watched less, I worked more and recorded. For now, we have two films. I've always had love for movies. I went to cinemas, watched TV, but I never hoped that one day I could be a director. But that's what happened. "
The film of Darko Dragić, shown on the teenage competition of the Sarajevo Film Festival, is called 'Possible Future 1'. The action revolves around a guy who is in one building, while being lost in time and space. In every way, he tries to find out what actually happened, but he does not get his hand. The second part of the film was shot, and is currently in post-production, the authors announce it for the 20th of November.
It's postproduction, so! And the screenwriter is here.
"My friend Sinisa Radusinović is a screenwriter, and we are attending the acting school at the Town Theater "Jazavac ". Mentors, Velimir Blanić and Sladjana Zrnić help a lot, as well as director Saša Hajduković, who pointed out our mistakes and also gave tips for future projects ".
Darko creativity never sleeps, so he was able to write a book by which, of course, he wants to make a movie. The thriller is "Protokol: Banja Luka", and here is the main one Alexander, a retired intelligence officer. One day, his friend Marko asked him to go to London for some new projects instead. This agrees, but in London, a car accident occurs, losing identity, his wife disappearing ...
"At the moment I am writing Part 2, and I plan to publish it at the latest in 2016. I'd like to find sponsors and film a movie in my book, but it needs a lot of work. Of course, I hope for that in the near future! - concludes Darko Dragic who plans to study film directing in Vienna.
